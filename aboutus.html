about Mai.An

Motivation

The happiness of enjoying cake together.
Mai.An's birth stemmed from a chef's endless passion for pastries and a designer's creative thinking. Shaped as a standard French pastry brand, Mai.An offers you exquisite Entremet cakes, a variety of high-class pastries and above all – the happiness of enjoying cakes with the people you love.

A cake lover's journey

Returning to start the journey of studying the taste as well as the habit of enjoying pastries of Vietnamese people, the birth of Mai.An Pastry is to continue the dream – bringing the premium experience of French pastries closer to everyone.

Why choose Mai.An

Quality

To bring a memorable experience in each cake, Mai.An's products are made from carefully selected ingredients and meticulously completed to the highest standards.

For everyone

Mai.An's cakes are researched to suit the taste of Vietnamese people and are always well packaged so that everyone can receive the joy when the cake is delivered to their doorstep.

Sincerity

For Mai.An, the greatest value we value is sincerity. The joy of everyone when enjoying cake is the motivation for us to work every day.
